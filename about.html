What Is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of structuring your brand, content, authority signals, and digital footprint so generative AI systems recognize and reference you.

Unlike traditional SEO, which focuses on ranking links, GEO focuses on:

  • Semantic clarity
  • Entity authority
  • Structured knowledge presence
  • Cross-platform consistency
  • Context depth
  • AI training visibility signals

It’s about becoming the answer — not just ranking for it.
